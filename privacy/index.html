Privacy & Cookies

1. Who we are

Ocure Analytics ("we", "us") is a boutique AI consultancy. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling data transparently and lawfully.

2. What data we process

  • Contact data (name, email, company) when you reach out to us.
  • Service data generated during projects (under contract and NDA).
  • Usage data (analytics cookies) only with your consent.

3. Why we process data

  • To respond to inquiries and deliver contracted services.
  • To maintain security and performance of our website.
  • To improve content and services (analytics — optional).

4. Legal bases

  • Contract performance and steps prior to entering a contract.
  • Legitimate interests (security, site operations).
  • Consent for non‑essential cookies and marketing.

5. Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary for the purposes above, or as required by law and contracts.

6. Sharing

We do not sell your data. We may share with vetted processors (e.g., cloud hosting, analytics) under data processing agreements.

7. Your rights (EU/UK GDPR)

  • Access, rectification, erasure, restriction, portability, objection.
  • Withdraw consent at any time without affecting prior processing.
  • Complain to your supervisory authority.

8. Contact

Email: privacy@ocureanalytics.com. If you’re a client, use your project channel for secure queries.

9. Cookies

We only use essential cookies by default. With your consent, we may use analytics and marketing cookies. You can change your choices below or anytime via the “Cookie preferences” link in the footer.
